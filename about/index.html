It Me

Hi! I'm Ngan, pronounced naan, like the bread. I'm a designer who loves code and I make things on the interwebs.

Currently, I'm at Vox Media working on the Branded Content Lab Team, leading design and helping build products & platforms that tell powerful and engaging stories both on- and off-platform for our brand partners.

In 2016, I was selected as one of 28 women out of over 400 applicants to attend the ONA-Poynter Leadership Academy for Women in Digital Media. DCFemTech also named me one of DC's Most Powerful Women Designers.

I graduated from George Mason University in 2012 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Art & Visual Technology and a concentration in Graphic Design. In 2014, I decided I wanted to learn how to code and develop my programming skills! I took General Assembly's 10 week part-time course in Front End Web Development and upon completion, I continued to utilize my newly acquired expertise and after some time, transitioned into a front-end designer position at Vox Media.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, cooking, traveling and making puns. You can e-mail me at knhoang13@gmail.com or find me on github, twitter, and/or instagram.
