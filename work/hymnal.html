Role: Design & Development

Before working on the Ad Platforms team, I was an avid user of Vox Media's proprietary custom display and native ad unit publishing platform, Hymnal as a developer on the Revenue Executions Team. On the Ad Platforms team, I was involved in a number of design sprints to create intuitive features and push the platform forward. My team members and I worked to develop a re-usable pattern library as well.
